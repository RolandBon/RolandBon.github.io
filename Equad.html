Equad

Welcome to the Equad Cafe! While Equad has the valid complaint that it is mostly just a giant block of concrete, you'd be surprised to find it has its own little charm. The Equad Cafe is a great place to spend time doing work or meeting up with friends, and if the weather's nice there's a nice courtyard full of plants and places to sit. And if you ever have an idea you want to make physical, Equad has all the tools necessary make whatever you could desire.

While you're here, you could hang out for a bit in the Cafe, grab a bite to eat or some coffee, or chat with some friends you saw doing work. Or, you could make some progress on your tasks and head to office hours. Or not, up to you!

Once you feel like you've spent enough time here (and hopefully got done what you needed to get done) it's time to choose your next path:

While the Equad Cafe may be nice, the food it offers doesn't compare to the great Whitman dhall, so you may want to head over and grab a bite before class.

If you're not really hungry but maybe want a more secluded spot to sit and do some work, it may be worth to check out some of the spaces in Frist Health Center.

Or... you could finally end your journey and make your way to class.
