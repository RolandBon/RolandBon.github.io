Equad

While Equad has the valid complaint that it is mostly just a giant block of concrete, you'd be surprised to find it has its own little charm. The Equad Cafe is a great place to spend time doing work or meeting up with friends, and if the weather's nice there's a nice courtyard full of plants and places to sit. And if you ever have an idea you want to make physical, Equad has all the tools necessary make whatever you could desire.

While the Equad Cafe may be nice, the food it offers doesn't compare to the great Whitman dhall, so you may want to head over and grab a bite before class.

If you're not really hungry but maybe want a more secluded spot to sit and do some work, it may be worth to check out some of the spaces in Frist Health Center.

Or... you could finally end your journey and make your way to class.
