Forbes Lawn

Even though it's off at a pretty far edge of campus, don't sleep on Forbes and its views. With its dining hall famous for its weekend brunch, Forbes also boasts a lawn that's a great place to spend some time. When the weather permits, the lawn is scattered with chairs facing across a scenic view of the golf course and Grad College, with plenty of sports such as frisbee, football, and volleyball ready to play. It may be hard to lose track of time here, but don't forget you still need to make it to class eventually.

If you're still killing time but would rather spend some time inside or the weather isn't cooperating, you could always head back to Butler and head to Studio 34.

Heading all the way to class might not be the move just yet, so if you're out of ideas it isn't the worst option to stop by Chris and Junny's room to see what's going on in there.

Stopping by Butler isn't always the most time efficient method, so maybe you want to make your way a little closer to class and drop by the Equad to do some work or maybe attend office hours for something.
