Meadows

Meadows is home to a lot of grad student housing, but also has a nice new facility with a gym and quiet spaces for you to relax or do work. It's also home to the Haaga House, which is the rugby locker room, and the fields we practice/play on. Meadows is highly underrated imo and is easily accessed by bus.

Since you're probably getting out of here by bus anyway, you should see what's around the bus stops. A decent option would be to get back on track and take the bus to Nassau street and finally make your way to class!

Or, you could keep riding the bus another stop and lock in on your office hours at Equad.

If you don't want to go all that way just yet, you could get off on Goheen Walk and spend some time in the scenic Frist Health Center.
