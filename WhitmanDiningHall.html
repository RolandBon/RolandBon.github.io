Whitman Dining Hall

Whitman's dining hall is combined with Butler's so that's usually the most convenient dining hall to hit. Some people like to talk down on Whitman food, but personally I think it's good enough, and if you're lucky enough they'll even have a make-your-own-burrito station for the week (only for lunch not dinner since budget cuts💔). You're sure to run into at least someone you know so grab food and catch up with someone for a bit before resuming your journey to class. First though, since you've just had a nice hearty meal, you might wanna work it off somewhere.

You could get some steps in and take a stroll around the Museum and appreciate all the works in there.

Or, you could side track a bit and take the bus to Meadows and maybe get a quick workout in at a usually empty gym.

If you think some fresh air would be nicer, you could also make your way down to Forbes Lawn for a bit.
