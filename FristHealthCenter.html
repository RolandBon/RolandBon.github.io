Frist Health Center

Don't let the name fool you, Frist Health Center is much more than an infirmary. Hidden throughout the building are many great spots to sit and do work or just relax and kill time. There are smaller rooms you can use or open spaces to chat, but either way you can find somewhere nice to stay in the building.

If you're in more of a relaxing mood, you could make your way back to Butler and hang out in Studio 34 for a bit.

If you're feeling like you've been cooped up inside too much lately then laying out on a lawn chair behind Forbes may not be the worst idea.

But if you've finally decided it's time to lock in and get the day going, you can make your way up campus and finally get to class!
