Chris and Junny's Room

My friends Chris (zee last year) and Junny (roommate in Romania Global Seminar) live two floors above me in WILF hall. Usually at least one of them or one of our friends is in there so it's a nice spot to chat, relax, and kill some time. They've got a TV, Xbox, snacks, and everything you could want, and they're door is taped usually meaning you can pop in whenever and see what's going on.

You don't want to spend too long in there though, sometimes it's a time trip, so from there you have a few options:

If you're in no rush and in the mood for a scenic detour, you could hop on the bus to Meadows.

If you want a change in scenery but still want to relax or play a game of pool you could go down to Studio for a bit.

If you're hungry or want to start making some progress towards class, you could stop over at Whitman Dining Hall.
